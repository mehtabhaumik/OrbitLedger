# Market Phase 46 Launch Polish

## Positioning

Primary promise:

> Collect faster. Close each day with confidence. Never lose track of who owes what.

Short store line:

> Daily money control for small businesses.

Plain description:

> Orbit Ledger helps small businesses manage daily dues, payments, invoices, statements, backups, and follow-ups without feeling like heavy accounting software.

## App Store Story

1. Today: see who needs payment follow-up first.
2. Get Paid: prepare reminders and promise-to-pay follow-ups.
3. Customer Timeline: remember dues, payments, notes, and reminders.
4. Daily Closing: finish the day with clear cash and follow-up checks.
5. Backup Confidence: know your business records are protected.
6. Web Workspace: clean up customers, invoices, reports, and exports on a wider screen.

## Screenshot Checklist

- Use demo data only. Do not show real customer phone numbers, emails, addresses, or balances.
- Show the mobile daily command center first.
- Show collection intelligence before invoice editing.
- Show backup confidence and daily closing as trust moments.
- Show web as the office companion, not the main mobile replacement.
- Check small Android, large Android, iPhone, tablet, and desktop web crops before publishing.

## Launch Trust Checklist

- Google consent branding: app name must show `Orbit Ledger` or `Orbit Ledger by Rudraix`.
- Public domain: launch domain should use Orbit Ledger branding, not a raw Firebase project domain.
- Privacy policy: must explain account data, local records, backups, app lock, billing, and support contact.
- Terms: must explain acceptable use, billing, refunds through app stores, data responsibility, and limitations.
- Support: publish support email and support topics for backup, restore, lock, billing, account access, and Google sign-in.
- Release notes: write user value, not internal engineering notes.

## Pricing Clarity

Free remains useful:

- dues and payments
- customer records
- basic statement exports
- backup and restore
- app lock

Pro adds presentation value:

- cleaner shared documents
- logo and signature branding
- Pro document themes
- future document templates and local review packs
