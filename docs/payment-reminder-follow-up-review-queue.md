# Payment Reminder Follow-Up from Review Queue

Orbit Ledger now lets owners copy a customer-ready follow-up message directly from the manual payment review queue.

This is intentionally simple for launch. It avoids pretending that Orbit Ledger sends automated payment reminders before notification permissions, WhatsApp integrations, and delivery tracking are ready.

## What This Phase Adds

- Copy follow-up action for manual payments in review.
- Pending, post-dated, deposited, and bounced payment messages.
- Invoice number included when the payment is matched to an invoice.
- Payment mode included when available.
- Bounced payment copy asks for an alternate payment without marking the invoice paid.

## Launch Rule

For now, Orbit Ledger copies the message. The owner sends it through WhatsApp, SMS, email, or another app.

Do not claim automated reminders are active until delivery tracking exists.

## Future Upgrade Path

Later this can become:

- WhatsApp share intent,
- reminder history,
- scheduled follow-up,
- delivery status,
- automatic reminder suggestions.
